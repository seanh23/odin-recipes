Ribs

This is my favorite way to prepare some tasty babyback ribs!

Ingredients

  • Fresh Pork Baby Back Ribs
  • Olive Oil
  • Sweet Heat Rub (or dry rub of your choice)
  • Brown Sugar
  • Squeezable Honey
  • Butter
  • BBQ Sauce (optional)

Steps

  1. Remove the membrane from the ribs (use paper towel to get a better grip).
  2. Apply the rub and let the ribs sit out while setting the smoker to 250 degrees.
  3. After about 15 minutes or so after applying the rub, place ribs on smoker meat side up.
  4. After around 2 hours, take ribs off the smoker.
  5. On some aluminum foil, apply some brown sugar, honey, and butter before placing the ribs meat side down on the aluminum foil.
  6. Put ribs (in the aluminum foil) meat down on the grill and let smoke for 2-2.5 hours.
  7. Take off the grill and let sit for around 30 minutes then enjoy!
