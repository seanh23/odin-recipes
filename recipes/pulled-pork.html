Pulled Pork

This is my favorite way to prepare some delicious pulled pork!

Ingredients

  • Boston Butt
  • Bad Byron's BBQ Butt Rub
  • Apple Juice
  • Salt and Pepper

Steps

  1. Trim some of the fat off of the butt.
  2. Season all sides of the butt and let sit for 30 minutes.
  3. Place butt on grill at 275 degrees.
  4. After 2 hours in, spritz the butt with apple juice and water.
  5. After 2 more hours, spritz again.
  6. 4 hours in, wrap the butt up in aluminum foil after seasoning and place back on grill.
  7. After 3-3.5 hours later (7.5 hours total) take the butt and let sit for 30 minutes to an hour and enjoy!
